# Sky Watch On Your iPad

## If You Have An Online URL

1. Open Safari on the iPad.
2. Type your Sky Watch website address.
3. Tap **Locate** once.
4. Tap **Allow** if the iPad asks for location.
5. Tap the share button.
6. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
7. Name it **Sky Watch**.
8. Tap **Add**.
9. Open Sky Watch from the iPad home screen.

Use the online website address from your host, such as Render. Do not use `127.0.0.1` on the iPad, because that only means "this iPad", not your computer.

## If You Are Using It At Home Only

1. Keep the computer running the Sky Watch server.
2. Make sure the iPad is on the same Wi-Fi.
3. Open Safari on the iPad.
4. Go to the home-network address for Sky Watch, not `127.0.0.1`.
5. Tap **Locate** once.
6. Tap **Allow**.
7. Add it to the home screen.

## Daily Use

1. Open **Sky Watch** on the iPad.
2. Tap **Chime on** if you want alerts for air ambulance, Coastguard or military aircraft.
3. Leave the iPad on the wall.
4. The display refreshes by itself.
